Fly Fishing Montana's Blue Ribbon Rivers

At River Run Guides, we offer unparalleled guided fly fishing trips. Whether you're a seasoned angler or just starting, our experienced guides will ensure an unforgettable experience. Safety, sustainability, and adventure are our top priorities.

Our Favorite Blue Ribbon Rivers

Madison River

The world‑renowned Madison cuts through dramatic canyons and meadowlands, offering rainbow and brown trout fishing almost year‑round. With our expert guides you’ll learn to read its ever‑changing currents and hook the hard‑fighting trout that made this river famous.

Big Hole River

Flowing from the Continental Divide, the Big Hole is a true wade‑fisher’s dream. Pristine headwaters, abundant hatches, and scenic backcountry access give anglers of all levels a once‑in‑a‑lifetime Montana experience.
